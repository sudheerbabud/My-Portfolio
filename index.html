Full Stack Developer

A full stack developer is a versatile programmer skilled in both front-end and back-end development, capable of building entire web applications. They work with a variety of technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React for the front end, as well as server-side languages like Node.js, Python, or Java for the back end. Full stack developers are valuable assets to development teams due to their ability to manage the complete workflow of web projects.
